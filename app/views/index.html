Get a passport in 1 week

You can use this service to:

  • renew an adult or child passport that has expired or that is about to expire
  • change personal details on your passport (for example your name, place of birth or gender)
  • replace a lost, stolen or damaged passport
  • apply for a first child passport

If you’re applying for a first adult passport, or you do not need a passport urgently, use the non-urgent service.

What you need to do

  1. Book an appointment online.
  2. Print and fill in your application form.
  3. Gather the rest of your documents before your appointment.
